## STS Logging

Here you can setup/enable STS Logging! The "STS Channel" will be the channel that the STS logs are sent to.
![sts](/images/stsloggingdb.png)

## Priority Logging

Here you can setup/enable Priority Logging! The "Priority Channel" will be the channel that the Priority logs are sent to.
